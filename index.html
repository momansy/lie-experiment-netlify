Image Description Experiment

This website randomly selects 10 image IDs from a pool of 50 images. The participant will assign 5 images to be described truthfully and the other 5 to be described deceptively. Then the website will show the images one by one and remind the participant whether to tell the truth or lie for each image.

By starting the experiment, you confirm that you agree to participate and allow your provided data, including your recorded responses, to be collected and used for this research project.
